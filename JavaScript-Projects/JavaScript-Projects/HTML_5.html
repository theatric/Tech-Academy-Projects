Celtic Tours

Adrian Metcalfe has been leading tours in Wales, Ireland and all over Europe for more than twenty-five years. He is a true Celt (an Irish mother and a Welsh father) and as such he loves stories and travel. He is a certified national guide in Wales and Ireland, a Welsh speaker, and is a great aficionado of the life and works of Dylan Thomas.

Aside from being a tour guide, Adrian works as an actor and got his start with Wales Actors' Company, touring castles and ancient sites around Wales with Shakespeare's Henry V. He lived in Vienna for several months while working for Vienna's English Theatre and the Kammerspielhaus; and then moved to New York where he worked for CBS's Community Concerts.

With bases in both Kinsale and Swansea, he can help you explore whatever parts of the Celtic region appeal to you, and whether it is one day or several, there will always be time to relax. In the words of one of the great Celtic saints, David: "Gwnewch y pethau bychain (Do the little things)!"...
